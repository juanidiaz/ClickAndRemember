About

This page is a game. The intention of the game is see if you can click on all the different images without remeating. Once you click on one image, all the cards will shuffle and will be presented in a different order. Then you need to click a different image; and keep going until you click on all 12 images. If you click on an image twice YOU LOOSE... Good luck!
